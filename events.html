Event Details and Documentation

Science Fair – January 2024

The Science Fair will showcase student projects related to environmental science, technology, and biology. It is an excellent opportunity for students to present their work and engage with experts in the field.

Sports Festival – February 2024

The Sports Festival will feature various athletic competitions, from track and field events to team sports. Students and faculty will come together to celebrate fitness and teamwork.

Cultural Night – March 2024

Students will have the chance to perform dances, plays, and music from different cultures. The night will be filled with color and creativity, celebrating diversity in our school community.

Graduation Ceremony – April 2024

The Graduation Ceremony marks the end of the academic year, where students graduate and receive diplomas. Family and friends are invited to celebrate the achievements of the students.
